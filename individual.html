Individual Therapy

Whether you are seeking specific support or engaging in self-discovery, I view therapy as a partnership and encourage ongoing feedback from my clients.

I offer individual therapy for adults (18 and older). My clinical areas of interest include anxiety, depression, ADHD, trauma and life transitions, including parenting and divorce. My therapeutic approach is flexible and collaborative, drawing from different modalities based on client need.
