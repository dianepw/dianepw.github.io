Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment with you?

I offer a free 15-minute telephone consultation during which we can discuss your needs and the therapy process. Just complete the inquiry form, and I will be in touch to schedule a call.

Where is your practice located?

1415 West NC Highway 54, Suite 201 in Durham, NC, located near 15-501 and I-40 for easy access to Durham, Chapel Hill, Hillsborough, and other Triangle communities. I also offer secure telehealth for clients throughout North Carolina.

Do you accept insurance?

I am in-network with most Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, and Cigna plans. Each potential client is responsible for confirming their benefits. For out-of-network clients, my rate is $150 per session.

Coaching and Family Resolution services are not reimbursable by insurance and are offered at an hourly rate of $165 for Parent and Co-Parent Coaching and $200 for Reunification Therapy and Parent Coordination.
