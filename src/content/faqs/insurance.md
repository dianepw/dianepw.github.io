I am in-network with most Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, and Cigna plans. Each potential client is responsible for confirming their benefits. For out-of-network clients, my rate is $150 per session.

Coaching and Family Resolution services are not reimbursable by insurance and are offered at an hourly rate of $175 for Parent Coaching and $200 for Co-Parent Coaching, Reunification Therapy and Parent Coordination.
