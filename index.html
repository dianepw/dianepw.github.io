Diane Pačes-Wiles, LCSW

As a psychotherapist, I work collaboratively with clients to clarify needs and bring lasting positive change. I have a passion for…

  • helping adults navigate challenges, build self-understanding, and create more meaningful, satisfying lives
  • guiding families through life transitions to build stable, sustainable relationships
  • empowering parents to understand their child's behavior and respond with confidence, clarity, and connection

I draw from various modalities to inform my work including cognitive-behavioral, mind-body, humanistic, and psychodynamic. But, most importantly, I emphasize my clients' personal strengths, encourage exploration, and build capacity to cope with challenges with compassion and humor.

Currently, I work as a therapist in Durham, NC. I welcome individuals from surrounding communities for in-person visits and throughout North Carolina via secure telehealth.
